Our mission is to activate and restore the Alger Theater and establish a welcoming destination for arts, entertainment, and education.

Activation

To enhance the quality of life through arts and culture, and to encourage community economic revitalization.

Renovation

To restore the Alger Theater as a staple of the community.

Destination

To preserve the Alger Theater and create a viable Performing Arts Community Center for the cultural enrichment of community.
